Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

Being a member of YeOrada is free.

To become a member of YeOrada, click the 'Register' button on our homepage and fill out the necessary fields on the page.

You can disable your account and all its data directly from our website by selecting "Settings > Deactivate Account". After entering your e-mail on the page that opens, click the "Deactivate Account" button.

You can change your password by selecting "Settings > Change Password". After entering your old password and new password twice on the page that opens, click the "Change Password" button.

You can't change your email address in YeOrada but you can open a new account with a new email address.

You can find the restorant you want in YeOrada using the "Find Restaurant" button with "Location" and "Restaurant name" filters on the home page of the YeOrada.

Your information (e-mail, phone number etc..) is not be shared with third parties in any way.

If you are the owner of a restaurant not listed on YeOrada, Click on 'Become a Partner' Button. On the page that opens, fill out the form and send to us. Once you send the corresponding form, our customer representatives will verify it and if everything is alright you will receive an email about confirmation.

No, you cannot order food or anything on YeOrada.

You can look into the rates and reviews written by the users on the restaurants profiles.
